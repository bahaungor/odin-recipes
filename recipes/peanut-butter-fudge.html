Easiest Peanut Butter Fudge

Description

This is the best recipe for creamy and delicious peanut butter fudge I have ever used. It is great for sharing at work.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 (16 ounce) package brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in brown sugar and milk. Bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. Remove from heat. Stir in peanut butter and vanilla.
  4. Pour over confectioners' sugar in a large mixing bowl.
  5. Beat until smooth; pour into an 8x8 inch dish.
  6. Chill until firm and cut into squares.
